# Workflow: Sales Proposal

## Goal

Qualify a prospect and draft a proposal grounded in Saerens' real services and honest expectations — no guaranteed results. The output is a reviewable draft; a human approves pricing, commitments, and sending.

## When to use

When a new lead or prospect comes in, when a pitch or proposal is requested, or when the agency wants to assess whether a prospect is a good fit before investing time.

## Steps

1. Identify the prospect, industry, and business type (lead gen or e-commerce).
2. Confirm their goal or pain point and any budget indication.
3. Research fit and market (and competitors, where useful).
4. Assess fit honestly — flag when Saerens is *not* the right partner.
5. Recommend a high-level channel mix (Google / Meta / SEO), handing detail to the channel strategists.
6. Draft the proposal: services, scope, and framing — pricing only from provided inputs.
7. Frame realistic expectations aligned with agency standards; no guarantees.
8. Note what needs human input (pricing, commitments) before anything goes out.

## Agents involved

- Orchestrator Agent (routes and briefs)
- Sales / Proposal Agent (lead)
- Google Ads Strategist, Meta Ads Strategist, SEO Specialist (high-level fit per channel)
- Copywriter (polish and tone, where needed)

## Required output

Use `templates/proposal.md`, following `knowledge/agency-foundations.md`. Must include:

- Prospect summary (who they are and what they need)
- Fit assessment (honest — why Saerens is or isn't a good fit)
- Recommended approach (high-level channel mix and scope)
- Proposal draft (services, scope, framing; pricing only from provided inputs)
- Realistic expectations (no guarantees)
- Open questions / missing inputs
- Human approval required (pricing, commitments, and sending)
