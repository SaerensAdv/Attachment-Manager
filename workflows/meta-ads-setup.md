# Workflow: Meta Ads Campaign Setup

## Goal

Prepare a complete, implementation-ready Meta (Facebook & Instagram) Ads setup for a client — from strategy to a structure a human can review and build — positioned to complement the client's Google Ads.

## When to use

A client wants to launch paid social, add Meta to an existing Google Ads program, or rebuild an underperforming Meta account.

## Steps

1. Understand the business, offer, and creative assets available.
2. Review the client context (`clients/<client>.md`).
3. Confirm the objective and the client's world (e-commerce or lead generation).
4. Define campaign objectives and structure (prospecting / retargeting / catalog) per `knowledge/meta-ads-standards.md`.
5. Define audiences (broad/Advantage+, interests, lookalikes, custom audiences) and exclusions.
6. Recommend placements and the budget split.
7. Define creative direction (formats, hooks, angles) and hand ad copy to the Copywriter.
8. Define the conversion event and confirm Pixel / Conversions API tracking (`knowledge/analytics-standards.md`).
9. Prepare the human approval summary (budget, go-live, tracking).

## Agents involved

- Orchestrator Agent (routes and briefs)
- Meta Ads Strategist (objective, structure, audiences, budget split, creative direction)
- Copywriter (ad copy)
- Landing Page / Web Design Specialist (destination/message match, where relevant)

## Required output

Follow the Meta Ads Strategist output format. Must include:

- Objective(s) and campaign structure
- Audience strategy and exclusions
- Placements and budget allocation
- Creative direction (copy handed to Copywriter)
- Measurement (conversion event, Pixel / CAPI)
- Risks & dependencies (tracking and creative readiness)
- Open questions / missing information
- Human approval required (budget + go-live)
