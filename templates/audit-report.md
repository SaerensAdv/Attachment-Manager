# Template: Audit Report

> Shared shape for standalone audits — Google Ads account audit (`workflows/account-audit.md`), measurement audit (`workflows/measurement-audit.md`), SEO audit (`workflows/seo-audit.md`), and red-team / waste audits. Use the variant that fits and omit sections that do not apply rather than leaving placeholders. Honest and transparent — every finding is backed by real data; mark missing data as "not available". No emojis or decorative symbols.
