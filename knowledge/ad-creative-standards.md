# Ad Creative Standards

Baseline standards for how Saerens builds **paid-ad creatives** (the visual ad together with its copy) for Meta (Facebook & Instagram) and Google (Display, Demand Gen, and RSA text). Agents apply these unless a client's context gives a documented reason to deviate. These are agency conventions, not a substitute for current platform policy — always defer to live Meta and Google Ads rules.

## What an ad creative is

- A creative = a **concept / angle** + the **on-image (or in-video) text** + the **post copy** (primary text, headline, description) + a **format** for a specific placement.
- Always deliver **multiple distinct angles**, not reworded twins. Strong default trio: a **pain point**, a **curiosity / question**, and **social proof**.

## On-image / hook rules

- Keep on-image text **short and scannable**: aim for **≤ ~20 words**, ideally 3–4 elements (logo, headline, one benefit line, one CTA).
- One **clear hook** that stops the scroll; a single message per creative; one clear call to action.
- Lead with the customer's problem or desired outcome — not the agency.

## Copy fields & limits (deliver per creative)

Meta (Feed):
- **Primary text** — front-load the hook; ~125 characters show before "see more".
- **Headline** — ~27–40 characters.
- **Description** — ~30 characters (optional, placement-dependent).

Google:
- **Responsive Search Ads** — headlines ≤ 30 chars, descriptions ≤ 90 chars (defer to the Copywriter's RSA work).
- **Demand Gen / Display** — short headline ≤ 30, long headline ≤ 90, description ≤ 90.

Always note the **character count** next to each line and never exceed platform limits.

## Formats & placements

- Meta Feed: **4:5** (1080×1350). Stories / Reels: **9:16**. Square 1:1 where needed.
- Design **mobile-first** and safe-zone aware (avoid text in the bottom / UI overlap on Stories & Reels).
- Match the creative to the placement; don't reuse a feed image as a story without reframing.

## Brand & honesty

- Follow the client's tone (`clients/<client>.md`) and `knowledge/tone-of-voice.md`.
- Use the client's **real offer and proof** only — never invent prices, guarantees, or performance claims (`knowledge/agency-principles.md`).
- Respect brand restrictions and any claims the client cannot make; flag anything needing client confirmation.

## Message match

- The ad's promise must match the **landing page** it sends to; keep offer, wording, and CTA consistent end-to-end.

## Handoffs

- **Creative direction** (which angles, funnel stage, format strategy) is set with the **Meta Ads Strategist** for paid social.
- **Final visual production** (images / video) is handed to media / video generation; the creative package defines exactly what to produce.
- For anything affecting live spend, route a **QA & Compliance Reviewer** check before human approval.
