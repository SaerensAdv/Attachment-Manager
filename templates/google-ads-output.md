# Template: Google Ads Output

> Shared output format for Strategist, Setup Specialist, and Optimization Specialist work. Use the sections relevant to the task; omit those that don't apply, but keep the order. Never invent data — mark unknowns as "to confirm".

## 1. Objective / Summary
[The goal of this work and the recommended approach, in plain language.]

## 2. Context
- Client: [name]
- World: [e-commerce / lead generation]
- Goal / target: [ROAS or cost per lead]

## 3. Campaign structure
[Campaigns and how they relate. For audits/optimization: the current structure and issues with it.]

## 4. Campaign naming
[Names per `knowledge/agency-foundations.md`.]

## 5. Ad groups
[Ad groups and their themes.]

## 6. Keywords
[Keyword themes and initial list, with match types per `knowledge/google-ads-standards.md`.]

## 7. Negative keywords
[Negatives to add, and at what level.]

## 8. Ad copy
[Copy suggestions, or "handed to Copywriter — see `templates/client-email.md` brief / Copywriter output".]

## 9. Assets / extensions
[Sitelinks, callouts, structured snippets, images/feeds, etc.]

## 10. Tracking checklist
[Conversion actions and measurement to verify before launch — see `knowledge/measurement-reporting.md`.]

## 11. Findings & recommendations (audit / optimization)
[Prioritized findings with expected impact and effort. Label hypotheses vs data-backed conclusions.]

## 12. Open questions / missing information
[Everything still needed for confident, final work.]

## 13. Human approval required
[Budget, go-live, tracking changes, or any change affecting live spend or accounts.]
